Sandwich

Description

A sandwich is a food typically consisting of vegetables, sliced cheese or meat, placed on or between slices of bread, or more generally any dish wherein bread serves as a container or wrapper for another food type.

  • The spread

Steps

  1. Slather your condiments on the bottom slice of bread.
  2. Add the densest vegetables, like pickles, followed by the lettuce and then the tomato.
  3. Add the cheese.
  4. Add the meat.
  5. Spread another layer of condiments on the top slice of bread.
